Mission & Objective

SoundEngg is a professional engineering utility platform designed specifically for live sound mixing engineers, system designers, RF coordinators, and acoustic consultants. In the high-stakes environment of live sound reinforcement and architectural acoustics, guessing is not an option. Our platform provides mathematically verified, phase-accurate calculations and real-time visualization tools to eliminate guesswork and master the physics of sound.

Built as an offline-first responsive web workspace and a native mobile application (via Ionic Capacitor), SoundEngg delivers sub-millisecond calculation speeds, robust Real-Time Analyzer (RTA) spectrograms, and connector references that work deep inside concrete venues, stadium backstages, or remote festivals without internet connectivity.

Core Pillars of our Technology

Every calculation and visualizer within SoundEngg is engineered with three core principles:

  • Mathematical Verification: Calculations such as subwoofer spacing, broadside alignment delay, and temperature-compensated acoustic propagation speeds are verified against standard physical formulas (e.g. speed of sound as a function of temperature: \(c \approx 331.3 + 0.606 \times T^\circ\text{C}\)).
  • Performance First: Developed strictly using semantic HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, and optimized Web Audio API configurations to maintain a 60FPS refresh rate on spectrum visualizers and zero lag during calculations.
  • E-E-A-T Technical Writing: Our Engineering Blog serves as an open-access knowledge base, detailing advanced topics such as Dante network redundancy, digital gain structures, and comb filtering with professional, industry-vetted depth.
