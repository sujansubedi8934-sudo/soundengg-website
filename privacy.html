1. Information Collection

At SoundEngg, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. We collect information in two ways:

  • Account Information: When you register via Supabase, we store your email address and profile details to sync your tool preferences.
  • Log Files: We follow a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected includes IP addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

SoundEngg uses Google AdSense to serve advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on this site.

  • DART Cookie: Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to SoundEngg and other sites on the Internet.
  • Opt-out: Users may choose to decline the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

3. Data Security (Supabase)

Your authentication data is handled by Supabase, a professional-grade database infrastructure. We do not store your passwords in plain text. All connections between your browser and our toolset are encrypted via SSL/TLS.

4. Third-Party Links

Our blog and tools may contain links to external sites (e.g., equipment manufacturers, technical documentation). We have no control over the privacy policies of these third-party sites.

5. Contact Information

If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at info@soundengg.com.
