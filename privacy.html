1. Information Collection

At SoundEngg, we prioritize the privacy of our visitors. We collect information in two ways to deliver a seamless audio engineering workspace:

  • Account Information: When you register via Supabase, we securely store your email address and profile preferences to synchronize your active toolkit setups.
  • System Log Files: Following standard technical procedures, we log system diagnostic metrics. This includes encrypted IP addresses, browser agents, Internet Service Providers, timestamps, and active page flows to debug calculator math.

2. Google AdSense & Cookies

SoundEngg implements Google AdSense to serve non-disruptive advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses industry-standard cookies to display relevant ads to professionals.

  • DART Cookie: Google's DART cookie uses analytical parameters to display custom ads based on visits to SoundEngg and external audio engineering sites.

3. Enterprise-Grade Data Security

Your authorization and authentication data are processed exclusively via Supabase, built on professional PostgreSQL database instances. We never store raw passwords or sensitive keys. All connections are secured under strict SSL/TLS encryption protocols.

Our dashboard and blog articles contain links to external resources, industry standard specifications, and manufacturer manuals. SoundEngg does not maintain control over, and assumes no liability for, the content or policies of third-party domains.
