## 2. Google Play Store Listing Draft

### App Title (Max 30 characters)
> SoundEngg: RTA & Audio Utility

### Short Description (Max 80 characters)
> The offline audio field companion: RTA, calculators, pinouts, and references.

### Full Description (Max 4,000 characters)
```text
SoundEngg Utility Kit is the ultimate, offline-ready field companion designed specifically for live sound mixing engineers, system technicians, monitor mixers, and AV crews. 

Tuning arrays, aligning delay lines, or wiring up a rack in the middle of a festival stage with zero cellular coverage? SoundEngg runs 100% offline, keeping essential calculations and references right in your pocket.

--- 🛠️ PROFESSIONAL FIELD TOOLS ---

• SPECTRUM ANALYZER (RTA):
Perform precise ISO-band analysis, peak tracking, and monitor frequency feedback in real-time. Calibrated for fast diagnostic checks on site.

• ACOUSTIC TIME & DELAY CALCULATOR:
Compute phase alignment offsets compensated for local temperature. Toggle between metric (meters/Celsius) and imperial (feet/Fahrenheit) instantly.

• CARDIOID SUBWOOFER ARRAY CALCULATOR:
Design cardioid spacing, d&b CSA cabinets, end-fire arrays, and broadside configurations in seconds. View physical wavelengths and delay settings instantly.

• CONNECTOR PINOUT REFERENCES:
High-fidelity wiring guides for XLR (3-pin & 5-pin), SpeakON (2-pole & 4-pole), TRS jacks, RJ45 networks, PowerCON, and DMX cables. No more searching manuals in the dark.

• SPEAKER IMPEDANCE & LOAD CALCULATOR:
Calculate speaker impedances (series and parallel loads) to ensure your amplifiers match and stay out of protect mode.

• SIGNAL GENERATOR:
Inject clean sine waves, white noise, pink noise, and custom frequency sweeps directly to your system for line-checks and room diagnostics.

• TAP TEMPO SUBDIVISIONS:
Find exact milliseconds and BPM subdivisions for delay units and effects parameters on the fly.

• EAR TRAINING FREQUENCY GAME:
Sharpen your ears on the road. Practice identifying frequencies, bands, and dB cuts to improve your live mixing adjustments.

--- 🎯 WHY SYSTEM TECHS TRUST SOUNDENGG ---

• 100% OFFLINE CAPABLE: Zero network dependency. Works inside concrete bunkers, outdoor festival fields, and underground spaces.
• BRUSHED METAL VFD DESIGN: High-contrast layout tailored for visibility in dark arenas and bright festival sunlight.
• NO INVASIVE PERMISSIONS: Respects your privacy. Microphone access is used purely for local real-time audio analysis.
• BUILT FOR THE CREW: Crafted by working system engineers who understand exactly what tools are needed during soundcheck.

Keep your settings calibrated. Download SoundEngg Utility Kit, your essential live sound field companion today!
```
